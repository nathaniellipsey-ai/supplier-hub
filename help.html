Walmart Verified is a badge that indicates a supplier has been audited and approved by Walmart's supplier verification process. This is a mark of trust and reliability.

Walmart Verified suppliers have:

  • Passed quality audits - Met Walmart's strict quality standards
  • Compliance certification - Comply with all relevant regulations and laws
  • Reliable delivery - Proven track record of on-time delivery
  • Business stability - Financial stability and sustainable operations
  • Ethical practices - Fair labor practices and ethical sourcing
  • Strong communication - Responsive and professional interactions

Why it matters: When shopping suppliers, prioritizing Walmart Verified suppliers reduces risk and ensures you're working with reliable, established partners.
