Getting Started

Welcome to Supplier Hub!

Supplier Hub is a powerful platform for searching, filtering, and managing supplier information. Whether you're looking for specific suppliers or want to maintain a curated list of favorites, this guide will help you get started.

First Time Login

  • Visit the dashboard and refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
  • Fill in your email address and name in the login modal
  • Optionally enter your Walmart ID for SSO authentication
  • Click "Login" or "Continue as Guest"
  • Your session will be saved automatically

Core Features

Search Suppliers

Find suppliers by name, category, location, or products. Use the search bar to quickly locate what you need.

Filter & Sort

Use advanced filters on the left sidebar to narrow down results by category, region, certification, and more.

Save Favorites

Click the star icon to add suppliers to your favorites. Access them anytime from the user menu.

Add Notes

Keep track of important information about suppliers by adding notes. Edit or delete notes anytime.

Compare Suppliers

Select multiple suppliers and compare them side-by-side to make better decisions.

Inbox

Receive notifications about your favorites, notes, and important supplier updates. Mark messages as read or delete them.

How-To Guides

How to Add a Favorite

  1. Browse suppliers in the dashboard
  2. Click the star icon on the supplier card
  3. See the "Added to favorites!" notification
  4. Access favorites anytime from the user menu

How to View My Favorites

  1. Click your user avatar in the top right corner
  2. Click "My Favorites"
  3. View all your saved favorite suppliers
  4. Click "View" to see details or "Remove" to delete

How to Add a Note

  1. Click on a supplier name to open their details
  2. Click the "Add Note" button
  3. Type your note in the modal
  4. Click "Save Note" to store it

How to View My Notes

  1. Click your user avatar in the top right corner
  2. Click "My Notes"
  3. See all your notes organized by supplier
  4. Click "Edit" to modify or "Delete" to remove

How to Check Your Inbox

  1. Click your user avatar in the top right corner
  2. Click "Inbox"
  3. View all your messages and notifications
  4. Click "Mark as Read" or delete messages as needed

How to Search for Suppliers

  1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
  2. Type supplier name, category, or product
  3. See real-time suggestions appear
  4. Click a suggestion or press Enter to search

How to Use Filters

  1. Look for the filter panel on the left side
  2. Click on filter categories to expand them
  3. Select the filters you want to apply
  4. Results update automatically

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to login? ▼

Yes, to use features like favorites, notes, and inbox, you need to login. You can either login with your Walmart ID or as a guest. Login is quick and takes less than a minute.

How long is my session active? ▼

For Walmart SSO users: 7 days. For guest users: 24 hours. Your session persists even if you close the browser or refresh the page. After expiration, you'll need to login again.

Can I share my favorites with others? ▼

Currently, favorites are personal to your account. They cannot be shared directly. However, you can manually share supplier information by copying the supplier name or details.

What happens to my data if I logout? ▼

Your data (favorites, notes, messages) is saved on our secure servers. When you login again, all your data will be there. You can access it from any device as long as you login with the same email.

How do I reset my password? ▼

We use Walmart SSO for secure authentication. If you're using your Walmart ID, manage your password through your Walmart account. For guest accounts, no password is needed - just login with your email.

Can I delete my account? ▼

For now, accounts don't need to be deleted - you can simply not login. If you need to delete your data, please contact support with your email address.

What's the difference between favorites and notes? ▼

Favorites: Quick way to save suppliers you like. Shows just the supplier name and date. Notes: Detailed information about suppliers. You can add custom text and edit anytime. Use notes for specific feedback or important details.

Account & Privacy

Your Data is Safe

All your data (favorites, notes, messages) is stored securely on our servers. Only you can access your account with your unique session token.

Session Management

  • Your session token is stored in your browser locally
  • Nobody else can access your account on this device
  • Sessions automatically expire for security
  • You can logout anytime from the user menu

Privacy

  • Your email is used only for account identification
  • No personal information is shared with suppliers
  • Supplier data is aggregated from public sources
  • Your notes are private and visible only to you

Need More Help?

Troubleshooting

Login doesn't work: Clear your browser cache and try again. Make sure your email address is valid.

Data not showing: Refresh the page (Ctrl+R). Check that you're logged in by looking at the user menu.

Pages loading slowly: This is normal for large result sets. Use filters to reduce the number of items displayed.

Can't find a supplier: Try different search terms or use filters. Check spelling and variations.
