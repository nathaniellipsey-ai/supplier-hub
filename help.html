ü§ñ UNDERSTANDING AI SCORE

What is the AI Score?

The AI Score is a proprietary algorithm that evaluates suppliers on multiple dimensions to help you make informed decisions. Scores range from 70-100, with higher scores indicating better overall performance and fit.

90-100

Exceptional - Top tier suppliers with outstanding track records

80-89

Excellent - Highly reliable and recommended suppliers

70-79

Good - Solid performers suitable for most projects

AI Score Factors

The algorithm considers multiple weighted factors:

Factor Weight Description
Performance History 30% On-time delivery, quality consistency, project completion rate
Customer Ratings 25% Average rating from verified Walmart buyers
Reliability 20% Years in business, financial stability, response time
Certifications 15% Industry certifications, compliance standards
Pricing 10% Competitive pricing relative to market rates
üí° How to Use AI Score: While a high AI Score is a good indicator, always review the supplier's detailed profile, certifications, and customer reviews before making a final decision. The AI Score is a starting point, not the only criterion.
